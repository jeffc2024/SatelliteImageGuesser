Welcome to SatelliteGuesser! The goal of the game is to guess the location of a randomly generated satellite image on the world map. Each round, a satellite image of a location on Earth is displayed. Click on the map to guess where you think the location is. The closer you are to the correct location, the more points you score. The closer you guess within 3000 miles, the more points you will earn based on your proximity by miles.
